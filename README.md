Building Elo rating systems with Monte Carlo simulation, chronologically validated win-probability models, and interactive analytics dashboards for NFL, NHL, and NBA.

## Projects

**[MultiSport Elo Lab](https://multisport-elo-lab.streamlit.app/)**  
Modular Elo + Monte Carlo framework for NFL/NHL with configurable adjustments (including Elevation Edge), parameter optimization, historical backtesting, and an interactive Streamlit dashboard.

**[NBA Win Probability](https://github.com/elevation-edge-sports-data/nba-win-probability/blob/main/nba_home_win_probability.ipynb)**  
NBA home-win probability models (Log5, logistic regression, RF, XGBoost) evaluated with chronological validation and calibration diagnostics.

**[NHL Playoff Team Stats](https://elevation-edge-sports-data.github.io/nhl-playoff-team-stats/)**  
Interactive NHL playoff dashboard combining 100+ years of regular-season, playoff, and advanced metrics (xGF%, CF%, FF%).

**[NHL Playoff Elimination Tracker](https://elevation-edge-sports-data.github.io/nhl-playoff-elimination-tracker/)**  
Animated map of every Stanley Cup Playoff elimination from 1918–2026 featuring season-specific logos and precise arena locations.
